About Us

Vietnamese cuisine is a wonderful blend of flavors, colors and aromas. With geographical diversity and rich cultural characteristics, Vietnamese cuisine has its own distinctive and attractive characteristics.

Vietnamese cuisine is not only about enjoying food, but also a way of life, a way of connecting with family and friends. It shows sophistication in the selection of ingredients and cooking, and is an integral part of Vietnamese culture. Try typical Vietnamese dishes and discover the wonderful flavors that this cuisine has to offer.

As a travel community, we believe that every experience is its own story, and we want you to be the writer of that story. We encourage interaction and sharing from our users, allowing you to ask questions and share your own perspectives and experiences.

Vietnamese cuisine is diverse and rich

Famous for the natural flavors and freshness of ingredients, Vietnamese cuisine uses a lot of raw vegetables, fresh spices and rich fish sauces. The dishes are often prepared simply, delicately and create a balance between elements such as salty, sour, sweet and spicy.

Pho, a soup consisting of fluffy noodles and beef or chicken soaked in a delicious broth, is a symbol of Vietnamese cuisine. Banh cuon, a dish consisting of a thin layer of cake wrapped in meat, mushrooms and onions, often served with sweet and sour fish sauce, is another signature dish.
