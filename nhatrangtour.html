Hanoi - the capital of a thousand years of civilization

One thing when introducing Hanoi - a very special Hanoi with many different cultures, and nowhere are there so many cultural villages like this place. Along with that, there are villages with Buddhist, folk, and French architecture scattered everywhere, giving visitors the pleasure of getting lost in a busy and developed city like Hanoi. cultural values ​​of thousands of years ago. Hanoi's thousand-year-old tradition of culture Hanoi's traditions exist from the smallest things, from saying "thank you", "sorry" to greetings and invitations. All have been agreed in educational standards so that everyone loves. That tradition is also reflected in traditional craft villages, streets selling unique products such as Bat Trang pottery, Hang Ma street, Hang Bac street, etc.

Tradition expressed in skills Religion, diverse beliefs Religion and beliefs have long become an important part of the spiritual life of Hanoians in particular and Vietnamese people in general. This land has many religions such as Buddhism, Taoism, Confucianism, Protestantism, Islam, Cao Dai, etc. to serve the needs of the people's cultural and religious life.
